6:30 AM: Start from Masanagudi

Begin your day with a peaceful morning drive from Masanagudi, where you can enjoy the fresh air and scenic views as you head towards the nearby attractions.

Moyar Road

Embark on a scenic 3-hour trek along Moyar Road, surrounded by lush forests and wildlife, including elephants and tigers. Enjoy breathtaking views of the Nilgiri hills and the Moyar River as you explore the diverse terrain.

Sigur Road (Sigur Ghat Road)

Take a scenic drive along Sigur Road, which is famous for its winding turns, stunning views of the Nilgiri hills, and lush forests. Watch out for wildlife crossings as you pass through this beautiful and remote stretch of road.

Vibudhi Malai

A peaceful hilltop Murugan temple offering spiritual calm and panoramic views of the Masinagudi forest valley. The climb is rewarding, surrounded by nature and a serene atmosphere.

Theppakadu Elephant Camp

Meet and observe elephants in their natural habitat and learn about forest conservation.

Kodanadu View Point
