Adaration Convent, Ooty

Start your journey from the peaceful Adaration Convent, surrounded by the cool mist of Ooty’s hills.

Botanical Garden:

Explore a wide variety of exotic and indigenous plants in this beautifully maintained historic garden. It's a perfect spot for nature walks, photography, and learning about flora.

Rose Garden:

Admire over 20,000 varieties of roses spread across terraced slopes in one of India’s largest rose gardens. The colorful blooms create a stunning visual treat and fragrant atmosphere.

Shopping:

Browse local markets for handmade chocolates, woolen clothes, and aromatic oils. Ooty’s street shops offer unique souvenirs and crafts at affordable prices.
